Study abroad

Where are you headed – and how long do you want to stay? The Global & Alumni Office offers different programs allowing you to study at international partner universities for periods ranging from one week to two semesters.

Stays via Erasmus+

With Erasmus+, you can study for up to two semesters at a partner university within the EU. You don't have to pay study fees at the partner university and you will get additional financial support.

Stays via TUMexchange

If you want to study outside the European Union, TUMexchange is the right program for you. The application process starts at the beginning of the winter semester.

Study in Switzerland

The Swiss-European Mobility Programme offers a three- to twelve-month stay at a Swiss partner university. Learn more about funding options, deadlines, and requirements.

Short stays within Europe

Taster week abroad: Through the ATHENS program you can stay at one of 24 partner universities within Europe for one week. Applications are possible twice a year.

Study within the EuroTeQ network

Through a joint course catalogue, TUM students can participate in online courses at the partner universities DTU, TU/e, L'X, TalTech, CTU, EPFL, and Technion.

Scholarships for global stays abroad

TUM Global & Alumni Office offers various scholarship programs for stays abroad and internships. Moreover, we can inform you about external offers, such as the programs of the DAAD.

School partnerships

Some TUM Schools and departments offer additional funding opportunities to those provided by the Global & Alumni Office. Inform yourself here about the options for your area of studies.

Stay abroad as a freemover

Besides the programs offered by TUM G&A, you can also organize a stay abroad by yourself. Those freemover stays may be registered in your Diploma Supplement as well. Find out about the requirements.

Double degree programs

Double degree programs with partner universities offer you the opportunity to study abroad and acquire a second degree in addition to your TUM degree.

Summer schools

A good way to spend your summer vacation: Summer schools provide valuable experience in general and are ideal to prepare you for a longer stay abroad.
