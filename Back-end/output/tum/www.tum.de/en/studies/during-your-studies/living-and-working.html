                  Living & Working

                  You are looking for an apartment or a place in a dormitory? You are in search of a part-time job? You would like support when starting a career or setting up a business? Here you will find comprehensive information and numerous offers regarding living and working.

                  Accommodation During Studies

                  TUM supports students with many tips, personal advice and its own housing exchange in the search for accommodation.

                  Jobs & Internships

                  For students and alumni looking for work or an internship, TUM offers a variety of different job boards.

                  Career Service

                  TUM’s Career Service supports students in all phases of their career planning.

                  Entrepreneurship

                  TUM offers extensive support for founders of technology companies.

                  Your contact persons for finding accommodation

                  Do you have questions about finding accommodation or about our support services? Here you will find your contact persons.
